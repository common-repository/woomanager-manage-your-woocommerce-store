== Description ==

With WooManager you'll be able to control your entire sales operations from the palm of your hand. Check order, make notes, call customers, get informed about low stock products, and much much more.

= Top Features =

* Product Management: supports simple and variable Products.
* Orders Management: change Order Status, create Notes and check order details.
* Dashboard: charts of current sales, low stock alert, and more.
* Multi stores: add more than one website to the App.
* Clients Management: call or email clients directly.
* Easy Connection: between your website and the App.
